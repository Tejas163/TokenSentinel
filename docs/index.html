How it works

Three steps from billing data to a prioritized savings plan.

1

Export your billing CSV

Download your monthly billing data from OpenAI, Anthropic, or any LLM provider. Most platforms offer CSV export.

2

Run the binary

Download, unzip, and run. Open localhost:8080, drag your CSV onto the upload zone, and click analyze. Takes 30 seconds.

3

Get your savings plan

Download a professional PDF report with prioritized recommendations, projected savings, and cost breakdown by model.

What you get

A complete cost governance toolkit in one binary.

📈 Cost breakdown

See exactly what you're spending per model and provider. Spot the outliers instantly in a clean table view.

Find cheaper equivalent models from any provider. Switch Claude to Gemini, GPT-4 to something leaner — without sacrificing quality.

Compare costs across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, and others. See exactly what you'd save by migrating workloads.
